Shehecheyanu

בָּרוּךְ אַתָּה יְיָ, אֱלֹהֵֽינוּ מֶֽלֶךְ הָעוֹלָם, שֶׁהֶחֶיָֽנוּ וְקִיְּמָֽנוּ וְהִגִּיעָֽנוּ לַזְּמַן הַזֶּה.

Baruch atah Adonai, Eloheinu Melech haolam, shehecheyanu v’kiy’manu, v’higianu laz’man hazeh.

Praise to You, Eternal our God, Sovereign of the universe, for giving us life, sustaining us, and enabling us to reach this season.
